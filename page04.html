Bidding Process 입찰방식

Submission of the Intention to Participate 의향서 제출 1. Submit an intention to participate by 18:00 on November 22, 2023. 1. 2023.11.22. 18:00 까지 입찰참가 의향서 제출
2. Upon submission of the intention to participate, deposit KRW 100 million into the K Engine Corporation's account by the time specified above. 2. 의향서 제출시 위 시각까지 1억원을 주식회사 K엔진 계좌에 입금

On the Bidding Day 입찰당일 1. Starting from 14:00 on November 24, 2023, fill out a bidding form at the bidding venue. 1. 2023.11.24. 14:00 부터 입찰함에 입찰표 투찰
2. Bidding closes at 14:30 on November 24, 2023. The highest bidder wins. 2. 2023.14:30 입찰마감 개찰실시 최고가 입찰자 낙찰
3. Within 10 minutes after declaring the winning bidder, deposit the remaining KRW 9.9 billion of the bid security deposit into the above-mentioned account. 3. 낙찰자 선언 후 10분 이내로 입찰보증금 잔금 99억원을 상기 계좌에 입금
4. In case of non-payment, if there is a backup bidder, the status of the winning bidder is automatically transferred to the backup bidder. The backup bidder must also deposit the remaining KRW 9.9 billion of the bid security deposit into the above-mentioned account within 10 minutes of being declared the winning bidder. 4. 미납시 만약 차순위자가 있는 경우 차순위자에게 자동으로 낙찰자의 지위 이전됨. 차순위자 역시 낙찰자 선언 후 10분 이내에 입찰보증금 잔금 99억원을 상기 계좌에 납입하여야 함
5. Immediate refund of the bid security deposit is made for unsuccessful backup bidders. 5. 낙찰받지 못한 차순위자들에 대한 보증금 즉시 환급

Final Payment 최종잔금납부 By 18:00 on December 22, 2023, pay the remaining balance (bidding price – KRW 10 billion) to the above-mentioned account and simultaneously transfer a 25% ownership stake (execute the share transfer agreement). - 2023.12.22. 18:00까지 잔금(응찰가 - 100억원)을 상기 계좌에 입금과 동시에 지분 25% 이전함(주식양도계약 실행)

Non-Refund of Bid Security Deposit 보증금의 미반환

1. The bid security deposit that is deposited simultaneously with the submission of the intention to participate will be forfeited if the depositor does not effectively participate in the bidding or if they do not deposit KRW 9.9 billion as the winning bidder on the bidding day. 1. 의향서의 제출과 동시에 납입된 의향서 보증금은 해당 납입자가 입찰에 유효하게 참여하지 않은 경우 또는 입찰 당일 낙찰자로서 99억원을 납입하지 않은 경우 몰수됨.
2. Bidders who do not pay the remaining balance until the final payment date will forfeit the previously paid KRW 10 billion. 2. 최종잔금납부일까지 잔금을 납부하지 않은 낙찰자는 기납부한 100억원을 몰수당함.
